About Manthan 2018

Global Connect @MANTHAN

MANTHAN is the ceremonious fest organized by PIMR which has evolved over the years as central India’s largest socio-cultural Festival. It is an amalgamation of entertainment, excitement, and showcasing the potential of the youth. Manthan has established itself as one of the most enthralling cultural festivals in M.P. Continuing with the tradition of providing an academically and culturally stimulating atmosphere, we endeavor to build on the grand success of MANTHAN'18 and expect to see a footfall of around 10,000 students from over 100 colleges and Universities from all over the world.
